Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026 · Effective immediately

Privacy Policy Terms of Service
Idea Vault is built with privacy as a default. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data on any server we control. Your ideas stay on your device.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Idea Vault ("the App"), developed by Pocket Micros, handles your information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of the following on our servers:

  • Your ideas, notes, or any content you create in the App
  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your location or device information
  • Your payment details (handled entirely by Apple)

3. Local Storage

All your ideas and sections are stored locally on your device using SQLite. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use the Cloud Backup feature.

4. Cloud Backup (Optional)

If you choose to enable Cloud Backup, your ideas are backed up to your personal cloud storage:

On Android: Your ideas are saved to a private, hidden folder in your Google Drive (appDataFolder). This folder is only accessible by Idea Vault and is never visible in your Drive file browser. We use Google Sign-In to authenticate your Google account. Your data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

On iOS: Your ideas are saved to your personal iCloud storage. iCloud is tied to your Apple ID and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

We never have access to your backup data. It goes directly from your device to your personal Google Drive or iCloud account.

5. AI Features

When you use AI features such as Organize Section, the content of the section you are organizing is sent to our AI processing service (via OpenRouter) for a single request. This content is not stored, logged, or used for training. The request is processed and the result is returned to your device immediately.

AI features are only triggered when you explicitly tap the AI action button. Nothing is sent automatically.

6. Analytics and Crash Reporting

The App may use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous usage data and crash reports. This helps us identify bugs and improve the App. The data collected is anonymous and does not include your ideas or personal content. You can review Firebase's data practices at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

7. Subscriptions and Payments

Idea Vault Pro subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. Subscription management, billing, and refunds are handled by Apple.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
